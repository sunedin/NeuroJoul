The Mission

To decentralize grid intelligence through autonomous agency. We deploy specialized AI Agents to act as the primary stakeholders of the low-carbon transition—negotiating, optimizing, and protecting energy assets in a real-time, machine-to-machine economy.

1. Autonomous Asset Agents (A3)

  • Every battery, inverter, and storage container is assigned a dedicated Neural Agent.
  • Self-Healing Protocols: Detect cell anomalies and reroute loads autonomously.
  • Edge-Inference: Site-local decisions deliver sub-millisecond fault response.

2. Multi-Agent Trading Swarms

  • Agent swarms replace traditional desks to optimize dispatch and market strategy.
  • Strategic Negotiation: Agents bid directly into Frequency Response and Reserve markets.
  • Game Theory Optimization: MARL predicts competitor behavior to maximize spreads.

3. Carbon-Proxy Agents

  • Agents align dispatch decisions with real-time carbon intensity.
  • Emissions Arbitrage: Shift discharge to displace high-carbon peaker plants.
  • Automated REC Management: Generate and trade RECs with verified provenance.

4. The Orchestrator Layer

  • The master agent governs hierarchy-wide control and policy constraints.
  • Balances trading swarm profitability against battery safety and longevity.
  • Maintains equilibrium between market performance and physical asset protection.
